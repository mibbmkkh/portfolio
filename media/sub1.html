ABOUT

Run, run, power up with super mushrooms!
Mario, who ventures into the mushroom kingdom using a variety of actions and items,
is the main character of Mario Bros, released for Famicom in 1983.
The Super Mario BROS is Nintendo's representative game,
which has been loved a lot as it continues to be a series.
THE SUPER MARIO BROS. MOVIE is based on the Super Mario Bros. series.

Based on the world of Nintendo’s Mario games,
the film invites audiences into a vibrant,
thrilling new universe unlike any created before in an action-packed,
exuberant cinematic comedy event.

The film is written by Matthew Fogel and directed by Aaron Horvath and Michael Zelnick.

STORY

Ta-dan-ta-dan-ta-dan-ta ♫

New York's ordinary plumber brothers, Mario and Luigi, are sucked into a mysterious green pipe while trying to save a city in crisis due to a faulty drain. In the process, his younger brother "Luigi" is kidnapped to the Darkland where Villain "Coupa" is located, and "Mario" decides to confront "Coupa" with the help of "Pitch" and "Kinopio" to save his younger brother. But before his powerful power to rule the world as a superstar, they are in unexpected danger...
